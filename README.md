# Analysis-of-Monetary-Policy-Announcements-and-Financial-Markets
Using Python script to  calculate  the AAR and CAAR of major financial markets to find determine the effect of Monetary Policy announcement in a specific time frame.
This studies the monetary policy announcements of the European Central Bank (ECB)
and the US Federal Reserve (Fed) and how they affected the major markets during the years
2020-2023. The period containing a pandemic shock and the following of a tightening cycle.
Using a standard event study design, this thesis implements a market model study over an
estimation window of 250 and 30 trading days before each announcement and evaluates the
abnormal returns on the day before, on, and after the announcement date. In addition, a hawkish,
dovish, or neutral classification rubric is implemented to analyze and determine the tone / position
of a monetary policy announcement released by the central banks in question. Analyzing the
financial market of assets : S&P 500, EURO STOXX 50, EUR / USD and Bitcoin.
